## 7. KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR PRODUCT-MARKET FIT

1. **Market is real and growing fast**: Emotion AI specifically is a $2.1-2.7B market (2024) growing at ~22% CAGR — reaching $9-13B by 2030-2033

2. **Definition matters enormously**: The 30x variance in market sizes comes from scope differences. For SaaS product-market fit, use the $2-3B narrow definition.

3. **Customer Experience Monitoring is the beachhead**: 27.9% of emotion AI revenue — the dominant application segment

4. **Driver Monitoring Systems are the fastest-growing**: 26.7% CAGR — driven by automotive safety regulations

5. **North America is the primary market**: 34-39% of revenue, highest willingness to pay, strong enterprise adoption

6. **APAC is the growth engine**: 25-34% share, highest CAGR, driven by government-backed AI initiatives in China, India, Japan

7. **VC funding is accelerating**: From ~$50-75M (2023) to $300M+ (2025) in identifiable emotion AI deals, dominated by Uniphore's mega-round

8. **Platform/API plays attract the most capital**: Hume AI ($50M for empathic voice API), Uniphore ($260M for enterprise AI platform) — infrastructure layer is being funded

9. **Smart Eye is the only pure-play public comp**: ~$38M revenue (2025), 14-21% growth — useful as a benchmark

10. **ML and Computer Vision are dominant technologies**: ML has 42.5% share; Computer Vision is fastest-growing — facial expression recognition remains the primary modality at 37% of EDR market
