01

Situation: Before TierPulse

Performance management was fragmented — no Tier 1, inconsistent Tier 2, and zero standardized Tier 3 leadership visibility

  • Tier 1 did not exist — no daily operational pulse at the shop floor
  • Tier 2 dashboards existed in silos — no unified escalation or action tracking
  • Escalations lacked consistent owner, due date, and status capture
  • No standardized Tier 3 leadership view across LVV sites
  • Manual data pulls, no governed metric definitions, no auditability
Design intent: Real-time operational pulse + structured escalation + transferable roll-up. Not dashboards for dashboards' sake — decision infrastructure.
