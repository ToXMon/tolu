05 / 08
Training Signal Design

Dual-axis multiplicative training signal

Score A × Score B = Composite

Multiplicative, not additive. Zero on either axis kills the composite. Technical correctness cannot compensate for emotional failure.

SCORE A · REASONING

Deterministic. Valid output, execution success, correction status, efficiency. Gold / Silver / Bronze / Miss → 1.0 to 0.0

SCORE B · EMOTIONAL

From four-channel multimodal fusion. Continuous -1 to +1, normalized 0–1. Spike events get 2.5× multiplier.
