02

Current Process Challenges

The manual drafting process creates risk, inconsistency, and delays

  • Manually labor-intensive task with no automation support
  • Lack of consistency in structure of instructions across authors
  • Missing details that can lead to patient safety risks
  • Human errors in drafting dosage and instructions — serious consequences for patients and company
  • Variability in wording and comprehensiveness across IPPIs
  • Multiple rounds of revisions and reviews needed to catch errors
  • Long cycle time — waiting on SMEs for first draft through back-and-forth revisions
  • Drafting requires years of experience — new hires struggle without a streamlined process
