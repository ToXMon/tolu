# Video Script Generator

## When to Use
Activate when asked to write a video script for YouTube, TikTok, Reels, Shorts, or any video format.

## The Process

### Step 1: Define Parameters
- **Format:** Long-form (YouTube) or Short-form (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- **Topic:** What's the video about?
- **Audience:** Who is watching?
- **Goal:** Educate, entertain, persuade, or inspire?
- **Tone:** Casual, professional, energetic, dramatic

### Step 2: Script Structure

#### Long-Form (8-15 min)
```markdown
## [Video Title]

### HOOK (0:00 - 0:30)
[Attention-grabbing opener. Best pattern: bold claim + preview]
[Visual cue: what's on screen]

### INTRO (0:30 - 1:30)
[Context setting. Why this matters to the viewer]
[CTA: Like and subscribe reminder]

### SECTION 1: [Topic] (1:30 - 4:00)
[Main content point 1]
[Example/proof]
[Transition to next section]

### SECTION 2: [Topic] (4:00 - 7:00)
[Main content point 2]
[Example/proof]

### SECTION 3: [Topic] (7:00 - 10:00)
[Main content point 3]

### CONCLUSION (10:00 - 11:00)
[Summary of key points]
[CTA: What should viewer do next?]

### END SCREEN (11:00 - 11:30)
[Call to action: subscribe, watch next video, link in description]
```

#### Short-Form (30-60 sec)
```markdown
## [Video Title]

### HOOK (0-3 sec)
[Pattern interrupt. Must stop the scroll.]

### BODY (3-45 sec)
[Key points rapid-fire]
[Visual cues for each point]

### CTA (45-60 sec)
[What to do next: follow, comment, share]
```

### Step 3: Add Production Notes
- [VISUAL]: What should be on screen
- [TEXT OVERLAY]: On-screen text
- [B-ROLL]: Suggested background footage
- [MUSIC]: Mood/tone suggestions
- [PAUSE]: Intentional silence for emphasis

## Constraints
- Hook must work in first 3 seconds
- Every 30 seconds should have a mini-hook (pattern interrupt)
- Write for spoken delivery, not reading
- Include stage directions for non-verbal moments
